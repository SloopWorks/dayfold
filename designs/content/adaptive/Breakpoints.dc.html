auto_awesome_motionDecision — how the fold behaves with two panes
The first unfold is a container transform: the tapped card's twin grows out of the list to fill the detail pane (~460ms, MotionScheme.expressive() spring) — the metaphor is intact, the card literally unfolds in place. Once a detail is showing, changing selection is a softer shared-axis fade-through inside the pane: the list never moves, so it never reads as a hard cut or a jarring shrink-then-grow. The source card holds a persistent selected state (tonal fill + 2px accent ring) the whole time. Back / dismiss: compact collapses the pane (predictive-back / interactive pop); medium & expanded clear the selection to the empty pane. prefers-reduced-motion swaps both for a plain cross-fade.
