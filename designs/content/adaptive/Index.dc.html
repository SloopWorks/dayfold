Adaptive content detail — two-pane
Dayfold · ListDetailPaneScaffold · ADR 0022 / 0009

When the screen is wider than a phone

The phone fold gesture is signed off; this is what happens at width. A list and a detail share the screen, so the fold can't grow to fullscreen — it unfolds into the detail pane. These four frames decide and show that behaviour across every Material size class, in light and dark.

The decision in one line: first pick = container-transform unfold into the pane; switching = shared-axis fade-through; the source card stays selected; back clears to the empty pane (collapses on compact). Walk through it live on Breakpoints.
