Scaffold nesting — why the fold survives

The shared element that runs the container transform is owned by the outermost SharedTransitionLayout. Because it wraps the nav suite, switching from bar → rail → drawer (or resizing across the hinge) re-parents the panes without unmounting the transition — so a detail that's open mid-resize keeps its identity and animates, never hard-cuts.
