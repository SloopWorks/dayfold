Avatars & hub people

Three deltas on the signed-off Settings + Hubs surfaces. A — profile avatars: monogram stays the default and universal fallback; a bundled, tasteful fun-avatar set sits in between (avatar_ref = bundled id, no upload, no external fetch — the ADR 0036 posture); photo is a visible, disabled M-next slot. B — roster: chosen avatars + quiet Owner/Adult role chips, owner and adult views. C — hub People (net-new core): the ADR 0030 allow-list made explicit and delegable — Family/Restricted toggle with a who-loses-access confirm, three plain-language roles (Viewer · Contributor · Co-owner) with an inline explainer, add/role/remove controls, and the owner/contributor/viewer/no-access capability boundaries. D — avatars in situ: hub-header stack + contributor bylines. Two-tier by design — no permission matrix. Names map 1:1 to Compose M3 · token parity with the design system.
